About Quizzo

Quizzo is a modern, interactive quiz platform designed to make learning and competition fun! Whether you're a trivia enthusiast, a student, or an educator, Quizzo offers a seamless experience for both quiz takers and quiz creators.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly, feature-rich environment where users can test their knowledge, track their progress, and challenge friends. Admins can easily add new quizzes, monitor user performance, and keep the content fresh and engaging.
